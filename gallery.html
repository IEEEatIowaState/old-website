Join Our Mailing List

Want to get involved? Here's what you should do:

1.) Click here to join the IEEE Iowa State Student Branch. You'll be placed on the IEEE e-mail list to be notified of upcoming events.

2.) Stop by at the next general meeting and get to know us! You can find information about general meetings on the calendar.

3.) If you have any questions about IEEE@ISU, send an e-mail to our president at ieeepres@iastate.edu.
