Welcome!

Welcome to the homepage of IEEE @ ISU! Here you can find information about our club and the projects we've worked on, as well as an up-to-date calendar of events. If you'd like to join the IEEE student organization, click the 'Interested in Joining?' button to find out how you can get involved. It's quick and easy to get involved, and joining the club is free!

Want to get involved?

1.) Email the President to join the IEEE Iowa State Student Branch. You'll be placed on the IEEE e-mail list and be notified of upcoming events.

2.) Stop by at the next general meeting and get to know us! Meetings are held Wednesdays at 7 in Coover 3043.

3.) If you have any questions about IEEE@ISU, send an e-mail to our president at ieeepres@iastate.edu.
